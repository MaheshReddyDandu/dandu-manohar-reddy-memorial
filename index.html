About Dandu Manohar Reddy

Dandu Manohar Reddy is the cornerstone of our family - a hardworking farmer who tilled the soil with his own hands to provide for us. His life story is a testament to the values of honesty, integrity, and unwavering dedication that he has instilled in all of us through his example.

As a father, he raised us with high moral values, never compromising on honesty and truth. He taught us that success comes from hard work, not from shortcuts or cheating. His simple yet profound wisdom continues to guide our family's path forward.
